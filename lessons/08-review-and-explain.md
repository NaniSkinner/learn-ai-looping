# Lesson 08 — Review and Explain

[← Previous: 07](07-verify-three-truths.md) · [Journey](../JOURNEY.md) · [Next: 09 →](09-preserve-the-lesson.md)

Time: 10–15 minutes

## Learn

If you cannot explain the change without the transcript, the agent performed but your learning is incomplete.

## Do

Close or hide the agent transcript. Explain:

- what changed,
- why this location was correct,
- which alternative was rejected,
- what evidence could falsify the claim,
- where you would look first after a regression.

Then reopen the diff and correct your explanation if necessary.

## Gate

- **Explain:** Complete the five-part teach-back.
- **Execute:** Review the diff independently.
- **Verify:** Identify any mismatch between memory and the actual change.
