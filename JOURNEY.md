# Foundation Journey

## Goal

Own one complete manual AI loop:

> Frame → Orient → Predict → Plan → Execute → Verify → Explain → Preserve

## How progression works

- Start with Lesson 00 and follow the numbered order.
- Complete as many lessons as your available time allows.
- Each lesson requires **Explain → Execute → Verify** evidence.
- Your instructor responds **Ready**, **Revisit**, or **Stretch**.
- Update `learner/progress.md` with your current lesson and exact next action before stopping.

## Graduation behavior

Given a small unfamiliar task, you can:

1. define the outcome and boundaries,
2. inspect the real current state,
3. make a prediction before seeing the agent's answer,
4. request and evaluate a plan,
5. authorize the smallest reversible execution step,
6. verify code, data, and decision claims,
7. explain the change without the transcript,
8. preserve one durable lesson without turning every observation into a rule.
