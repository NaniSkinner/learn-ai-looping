# Lesson 09 — Preserve the Lesson

[← Previous: 08](08-review-and-explain.md) · [Journey](../JOURNEY.md) · [Next: 10 →](10-capstone.md)

Time: 10–15 minutes

## Learn

Do not preserve a transcript. Preserve the smallest durable delta that should affect a future run.

## Do

Record:

- lesson,
- supporting evidence,
- future trigger,
- boundary or counterexample,
- destination: nowhere, progress note, repository guidance, reusable workflow, or mechanical gate.

Do not edit `AGENTS.md` unless the lesson represents a repeated repository need.

## Gate

- **Explain:** Why is “save everything” a poor memory strategy?
- **Execute:** Write one bounded lesson record.
- **Verify:** Defend its destination and name what you deliberately did not preserve.
